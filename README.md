# ExportPath AI

### From farm to market, without the guesswork.

ExportPath AI is an AI-powered export advisory platform designed to help smallholder farmers access export markets with confidence.

Built for the Kenya AI Challenge 2026 (Mercy Corps AgriFin Track), ExportPath AI helps farmers identify compliance risks, understand export requirements, access logistics services, and discover financial protection options before costly mistakes occur.

## Problem

Kenya exports more than USD 1.2 billion worth of horticultural produce annually, with over 80% produced by smallholder farmers.

Many farmers have limited visibility into export requirements and often rely on fragmented information when making decisions about:

* Fertilizers and pesticides
* Certification
* Logistics
* Cold chain transport
* Insurance
* Export documentation

Mistakes made at the farm level can result in rejected consignments, lost income, and exclusion from export markets.

## Solution

ExportPath AI provides four integrated services:

### 1. Compliance Screening

Farmers photograph fertilizer or pesticide labels.

The platform:

* Extracts ingredient information
* Identifies restricted substances
* Checks against EU regulations
* Explains risks in plain language
* Suggests safer alternatives

### 2. Export Readiness Advisory

Personalized recommendations based on:

* Crop
* Target market
* Certification status
* Compliance requirements

### 3. Equipment & Logistics Access

Farmers can discover:

* Tractor services
* Harvesting services
* Refrigerated transport
* Aggregation and export logistics options

### 4. Insurance Guidance

The platform recommends:

* Crop insurance
* Cargo insurance
* Transit cover
* Subsidized agricultural insurance programs

## AI Components

* OCR for fertilizer and pesticide labels
* Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
* Regulatory knowledge retrieval
* Personalized advisory recommendations

## Neo4j Knowledge Graph

Neo4j powers the relationships between:

Farmer → Crop → Input → Substance → Regulation → Export Risk

and

Crop → Market → Certification → Logistics → Insurance

The graph enables explainable recommendations and GraphRAG retrieval.
